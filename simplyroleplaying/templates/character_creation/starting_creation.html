Creating a character in the Simply Roleplaying system is incredibly easy. One simply must follow these four steps in no particular order.

  1. Set up your character's background - pick a character name, a personality and a reasonable backstory for your character. The personality and backstory should make sense together, and should reflect the points you put in the stats you picked earlier. Remember; the more detail, the more fun!
  2. Select a race for your character to be, ask your GM for the list of races in their world. The default races can be found by clicking here.
  3. Set up your stats. Allocate your creation points to determine what die you will roll for that stat.
