Creating a character in the Simply Roleplaying system is incredibly easy. One simply must follow these four steps.

  1. Pick a character name - Use your own if you'd like!
  2. Select a race for your character to be, ask your GM for the list of races or check out the provided races by clicking here.
  3. Allocate points to each stat, to determine what die you will roll for that stat.
  4. Optional: Select advantages and disadvantages for your character to start with. (Talk with your GM if they will allow advantages and disadvantages in their campaign)
  5. Pick a personality and a reasonable backstory for your character. The personality and backstory should make sense together, and should reflect the points you put in the stats you picked earlier. (Remember: the more detail, the more fun!)
